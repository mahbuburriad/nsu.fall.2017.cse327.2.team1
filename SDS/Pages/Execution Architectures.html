5.0.Execution Architectures

Calls to the database should not take longer than (but should be much less than) 30 seconds and transfers of the each Object over the network should not take longer than 1 minute. Conversion of a ResultSet to a PHP Object should take less than 1 second. Data retrievals in general should be less than 2MB.
